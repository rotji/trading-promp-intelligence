# IMPORTANT PRINCIPLE

GBPUSD is primarily driven by the relative strength of the British Pound versus the US Dollar.

The highest-quality opportunities usually occur when:

• Federal Reserve policy

• Bank of England policy

• Interest rate differentials

• Dollar liquidity

• Global capital flows

• Chart structure

are aligned in the same direction.
