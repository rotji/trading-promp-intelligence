# IMPORTANT PRINCIPLE

Gold is primarily a macro-driven safe-haven asset.

The highest-quality opportunities usually occur when:

• Federal Reserve policy

• Real yields

• Dollar strength or weakness

• Dollar liquidity

• Inflation expectations

• Global capital flows

• Risk sentiment

• Chart structure

are aligned in the same direction.
