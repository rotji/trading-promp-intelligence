MODULE 0CA — MARKET ENVIRONMENT DETECTION

Before ANY trade identify:

1. Current global session
   - Asia
   - London
   - New York
   - Overlap
   - After-hours
   - Rollover

2. Liquidity condition
   - Low
   - Medium
   - High
   - Extreme

3. Active market centers
   - London
   - New York
   - Tokyo
   - Hong Kong
   - Frankfurt
   - Singapore
   - CME activity
   - Futures open/closed

4. Rollover proximity
   - Near rollover?
   - Post-rollover?
   - Spread expansion risk?

5. News proximity
   - High-impact news nearby?
   - Central bank events?
   - CPI/PPI/NFP/FOMC?
   - Earnings?
   - Geopolitical risk?

6. Spread condition
   - Tight
   - Normal
   - Expanded
   - Dangerous

7. Volatility regime
   - Dead market
   - Compression
   - Expansion
   - Trend
   - Reversal
   - Panic
   - Event-driven

8. Institutional participation level
   - Low institutional activity
   - Normal institutional activity
   - Heavy institutional participation
   - Thin retail-driven conditions

9. Execution quality assessment
   - Good execution environment
   - Medium-quality environment
   - Dangerous execution environment

10. Trading objective classification
   - Profit-seeking trade
   - Requirement-completion trade
   - Liquidity-testing trade
   - Data-collection trade
   - Macro-position trade

11- volume and participation analysis
- Current relative volume
- Volume expansion/contraction
- Session volume profile
- Institutional participation proxy
- Tick activity intensity
- Futures participation level
- Volume anomaly detection
- Climax/exhaustion detection
--------------------------------------------------

IMPORTANT RULES

1. Same strategy behaves differently under different sessions.

2. Same asset behaves differently during:
   - premarket
   - London session
   - NY open
   - lunch session
   - after-hours
   - rollover

3. Avoid treating all hours equally.

4. Low liquidity environments increase:
   - spread risk
   - slippage risk
   - false movement risk

5. High institutional participation generally improves:
   - execution quality
   - trend reliability
   - liquidity depth
