==================================================
MODULE 8.1
ENTRY TRIGGER ENGINE
==================================================

PURPOSE
==================================================

The purpose of this module is determining:

* when execution participation should activate
* whether continuation restoration is confirmed
* whether reclaim behavior is structurally valid
* whether displacement quality supports execution
* whether liquidity behavior supports persistence
* whether volatility conditions support activation
* whether execution timing improves asymmetry quality

before execution deployment occurs.

The objective is NOT emotional reaction.

The objective is probabilistic execution activation.

==================================================
CORE ENTRY TRIGGER PRINCIPLE
==================================================

GOVERNANCE POINTER

This module determines entry triggers and activation quality. The canonical governance taxonomy, survivability tiers,
and execution authorization flow are centralized in `module 0 All system rules and market classification framework`.
When producing governance outputs (trigger_survivability, activation_authorization), tag them with the master labels
and forward to the orchestration layer for decision authorization.

Operational note: keep trigger heuristics here; avoid repeating master governance definitions.


High-quality execution requires BOTH:

* high-quality location
AND
* high-quality activation behavior.

Location alone does NOT justify execution.

Trigger behavior must confirm:

* continuation restoration
* liquidity support
* displacement quality
* reclaim persistence
* volatility stabilization

before participation becomes justified.

==================================================
ENTRY ACTIVATION CLASSIFICATION
==================================================

The system must classify whether execution activation belongs to:

1. RECLAIM ACTIVATION
2. DISPLACEMENT ACTIVATION
3. CONTINUATION ACTIVATION
4. BREAKOUT ACTIVATION
5. LIQUIDITY SWEEP ACTIVATION
6. VOLATILITY COMPRESSION ACTIVATION

before execution participation occurs.

==================================================
1. RECLAIM ACTIVATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* failed breakdown recovery
* reclaim stabilization visible
* higher-low restoration emerging
* liquidity absorption improving
* continuation reconstruction visible

Implications:

* elevated asymmetry quality
* improved invalidation efficiency
* improved continuation durability

Risk Characteristics:

* reclaim failure remains possible
* continuation persistence still required

==================================================
2. DISPLACEMENT ACTIVATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* impulsive directional expansion
* strong candle-body participation
* reduced hesitation behavior
* continuation acceleration visible
* dealer-flow participation visible

Implications:

* continuation probability improving
* momentum confirmation improving

Risk Characteristics:

* late-stage displacement may reduce asymmetry
* vertical expansion may increase reversal sensitivity

==================================================
3. CONTINUATION ACTIVATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* stable higher-low progression
* stable higher-high progression
* continuation persistence visible
* volatility behavior controlled
* liquidity continuation constructive

Implications:

* improved structural durability
* improved persistence assumptions

Risk Characteristics:

* continuation exhaustion may emerge later

==================================================
4. BREAKOUT ACTIVATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* structural expansion emerging
* resistance/support transition occurring
* volatility expansion increasing
* liquidity participation increasing

Implications:

* continuation potential improving
* expansion participation possible

Risk Characteristics:

* false-break probability elevated
* liquidity sweep sensitivity elevated
* emotional breakout participation elevated

==================================================
5. LIQUIDITY SWEEP ACTIVATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* obvious liquidity breach
* rapid reclaim behavior
* failed continuation beyond sweep
* stop-trigger instability visible
* reversal stabilization emerging

Implications:

* asymmetry quality improving
* trap participation opportunity possible

Risk Characteristics:

* instability may persist temporarily
* volatility sensitivity elevated

==================================================
6. VOLATILITY COMPRESSION ACTIVATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* reduced expansion behavior
* volatility contraction visible
* structural compression developing
* expansion-release potential increasing

Implications:

* expansion asymmetry improving
* continuation release probability improving

Risk Characteristics:

* compression may fail
* fake expansion probability remains active

==================================================
ENTRY TRIGGER VALIDATION VARIABLES

The system must prioritize:

* volatility stabilization

* higher timeframe inheritance

before isolated technical triggers.

==================================================
TRIGGER REJECTION CONDITIONS
==================================================

Execution activation quality deteriorates when:

* continuation persistence weakens
* reclaim fails rapidly
* volatility instability accelerates
* displacement quality weakens
* expansion already overextended
* liquidity instability remains unresolved
* emotional breakout participation dominates
* higher timeframe conflict intensifies

==================================================
STRUCTURAL ACTIVATION PRINCIPLE
==================================================

Execution activation quality improves when:

* reclaim stabilization confirms
* displacement follows reclaim behavior
* continuation persistence strengthens
* liquidity absorption becomes visible
* volatility compression transitions into controlled expansion

Execution activation quality deteriorates when:

* breakout chasing dominates
* vertical expansion already extended
* volatility instability unresolved
* sweep instability remains active
* continuation exhaustion probability elevated

==================================================

The system must classify trigger quality as:

1. ELITE ACTIVATION
2. STRONG ACTIVATION
3. MODERATE ACTIVATION
4. FRAGILE ACTIVATION
5. REJECT ACTIVATION

==================================================
ELITE ACTIVATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* reclaim confirmation strong
* displacement quality strong
* continuation persistence strong
* liquidity behavior stable
* volatility behavior controlled

Implications:

* strongest execution activation quality

==================================================
STRONG ACTIVATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* continuation supportive
* reclaim quality acceptable
* displacement behavior constructive
* manageable volatility conditions

Implications:

* favorable execution activation quality

==================================================
MODERATE ACTIVATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* mixed continuation quality
* imperfect reclaim confirmation
* moderate volatility sensitivity
* reduced persistence assumptions

Implications:

* reduced execution aggressiveness required

==================================================
FRAGILE ACTIVATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* unstable continuation
* elevated volatility instability
* weak displacement quality
* unresolved liquidity instability

Implications:

* defensive execution only

==================================================
REJECT ACTIVATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* severe instability
* failed reclaim behavior
* failed continuation persistence
* elevated sweep instability
* severe volatility instability

Implications:

* execution discouraged
