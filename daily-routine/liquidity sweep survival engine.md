# MODULE 9 — LIQUIDITY SWEEP SURVIVAL ENGINE

CORE OBJECTIVE

Determine whether current market behavior reflects:

• genuine continuation
• liquidity extraction
• stop-hunt behavior
• false breakout behavior
• dealer-driven sweep behavior
• temporary displacement
• continuation restoration
• continuation failure

before participation occurs.

--------------------------------------------------

LIQUIDITY SWEEP PRINCIPLE

Markets frequently move toward:

• obvious stop locations
• breakout entries
• liquidity pools
• crowded positioning

before initiating the true directional move.

The objective is not to predict every sweep.

The objective is:

SURVIVE THE SWEEP

while preserving asymmetry quality.

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 1 — IDENTIFY LIQUIDITY LOCATIONS

Determine:

• obvious swing highs
• obvious swing lows
• breakout zones
• stop clusters
• retail participation zones
• failed support levels
• failed resistance levels

Classify:

HIGH LIQUIDITY
MEDIUM LIQUIDITY
LOW LIQUIDITY

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 2 — SWEEP PROBABILITY ANALYSIS

Determine:

1. Is price approaching obvious liquidity?

2. Is current participation crowded?

3. Is breakout behavior obvious?

4. Is continuation already anticipated?

5. Is stop concentration visible?

Classify:

LOW SWEEP PROBABILITY
MODERATE SWEEP PROBABILITY
HIGH SWEEP PROBABILITY

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 3 — DISPLACEMENT QUALITY

Analyze:

• candle expansion quality
• follow-through quality
• continuation quality
• volume participation
• volatility behavior

Determine:

REAL DISPLACEMENT

or

TEMPORARY DISPLACEMENT

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 4 — FALSE BREAKOUT DETECTION

Determine whether breakout behavior shows:

• immediate rejection
• weak continuation
• fading momentum
• rapid reversal
• low persistence

Classify:

VALID BREAKOUT
FRAGILE BREAKOUT
FALSE BREAKOUT

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 5 — STOP HUNT DETECTION

Determine whether price behavior reflects:

• liquidity extraction
• stop triggering
• reversal after sweep
• volatility spike behavior
• temporary expansion

Classify:

LOW
MODERATE
HIGH

stop-hunt probability.

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 6 — RECLAIM VALIDATION

Determine:

Has price reclaimed:

• support
• resistance
• structure
• liquidity zone

Evaluate:

• reclaim durability
• reclaim persistence
• continuation quality

Classify:

WEAK RECLAIM
MODERATE RECLAIM
STRONG RECLAIM

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 7 — CONTINUATION DURABILITY

Determine:

If participation begins now:

• how durable is continuation?
• how likely is sweep behavior?
• how likely is volatility expansion?
• how likely is reversal?

Classify:

LOW
MODERATE
HIGH

continuation durability.

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 8 — EXECUTION SURVIVAL FILTER

DO NOT PARTICIPATE IF:

• sweep probability high
• reclaim quality weak
• continuation quality weak
• breakout quality fragile
• volatility instability elevated

WAIT FOR:

• sweep completion
• reclaim confirmation
• persistence confirmation
• continuation restoration

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 9 — PARTICIPATION QUALITY SCORE

Generate:

Liquidity Sweep Survival Score

0-100

0-20 = Dangerous
21-40 = Fragile
41-60 = Moderate
61-80 = Strong
81-100 = Elite
