# MODULE 14 — CONTINUATION DURABILITY ENGINE

========================================================
CORE OBJECTIVE
========================================================

Determine:

• whether continuation remains healthy
• whether continuation is weakening
• whether continuation is deteriorating
• whether continuation is exhausted
• whether continuation expectancy remains favorable

The objective is NOT:

• predicting every move
• assuming trends last forever
• forcing continuation trades

The objective is:

• measuring continuation durability
• measuring persistence quality
• measuring expansion quality
• measuring asymmetry sustainability

--------------------------------------------------------

UNIVERSAL PRINCIPLE

A trend can exist
while continuation is already dying.

A continuation trade should only be taken when:

CONTINUATION QUALITY
REMAINS DURABLE

========================================================
PRIMARY QUESTION
========================================================

Before every trade ask:

IS THE MARKET STILL WILLING
TO CONTINUE?

NOT:

HAS THE MARKET MOVED BEFORE?

========================================================
CONTINUATION HEALTH ENGINE
========================================================

========================================================
PERSISTENCE ENGINE
========================================================

Question:

After moving,
does price continue moving?

Or immediately reverse?

Strong Persistence:

• continuation candles
• minimal retracement
• sustained pressure

Weak Persistence:

• immediate rejection
• rapid reversal
• overlapping candles

Score:

0–10

========================================================
EXPANSION QUALITY ENGINE
========================================================

Question:

Are expansions becoming stronger?

Or weaker?

Strong Expansion:

• large displacement candles
• strong directional intent
• healthy follow-through

Weak Expansion:

• shrinking candles
• hesitation
• repeated stalls

Score:

0–10

========================================================
RECLAIM DURABILITY ENGINE
========================================================

Question:

When price reclaims a level:

Does it hold?

Or fail quickly?

Strong Reclaim:

• hold above reclaim
• follow-through continuation
• buyer/seller commitment

Weak Reclaim:

• reclaim failure
• immediate rejection
• false continuation

Score:

0–10

========================================================
FOLLOW-THROUGH ENGINE
========================================================

Question:

Does continuation receive confirmation?

Or does momentum disappear?

Strong Follow-Through:

• continuation after breakout
• continuation after reclaim
• continuation after pullback

Weak Follow-Through:

• hesitation
• repeated rejection
• immediate reversal

Score:

0–10

========================================================
MOMENTUM DURABILITY ENGINE

• acceleration

Weak Momentum:

Score:

0–10

========================================================
CONTINUATION DETERIORATION ENGINE
========================================================

• shrinking expansions

Warning:

Continuation Deterioration Active

========================================================

========================================================

• diminishing expansion
• diminishing follow-through
• increasing rejection

continuation quality weakening

while continuation weakens.

while continuation deteriorates.

Direction can remain correct

while continuation expectancy collapses.

Therefore:

Continuation must be evaluated independently.
