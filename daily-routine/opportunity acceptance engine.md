# MODULE 16 — OPPORTUNITY ACCEPTANCE ENGINE

========================================================
CORE OBJECTIVE
========================================================

Determine whether an opportunity deserves capital allocation.

The objective is NOT:

• finding trades
• maximizing trade frequency
• forcing participation

The objective IS:

• rejecting weak opportunities
• accepting strong opportunities
• preserving capital
• maximizing expectancy
• maximizing asymmetry
• improving survivability

--------------------------------------------------------

UNIVERSAL PRINCIPLE

A market can be:

• structurally bullish
• environmentally healthy
• timing aligned

AND STILL BE

A POOR OPPORTUNITY.

The objective is not:

FIND MORE TRADES

The objective is:

ACCEPT BETTER TRADES

========================================================
PRIMARY QUESTION
========================================================

Before every trade ask:

DOES THIS OPPORTUNITY DESERVE CAPITAL?

NOT:

CAN I FIND A REASON TO ENTER?

========================================================
OPPORTUNITY ACCEPTANCE PHILOSOPHY
========================================================

Professional traders:

Reject most opportunities.

Retail traders:

Accept most opportunities.

The system must learn:

Capital is deployed only when:

• asymmetry exists
• durability exists
• continuation exists
• participation exists
• expectancy exists

========================================================
AUTOMATIC REJECTION RULES
========================================================

Reject if:

• Environment Tier 5

• Continuation failing

• Participation failing

• Liquidity sweep probability extreme

• Event risk extreme

• Expectancy weak

• Asymmetry poor

• Structure directly opposes trade

--------------------------------------------------------

Even if:

Direction appears correct.

========================================================
FOMO DETECTION ENGINE
========================================================

• chasing
• emotional urgency
• fear of missing out
• late participation

Questions:

Possible FOMO participation.

========================================================
SURVIVABILITY ENGINE
========================================================

Questions:

Can this trade damage survivability?

Can this trade damage continuity?

Can this trade damage discipline?

--------------------------------------------------------

Capital preservation has priority over opportunity frequency.

========================================================
POST-TRADE OPPORTUNITY REVIEW
========================================================

Determine:

1. Was opportunity quality correctly classified?

2. Was acceptance justified?

3. Should opportunity have been rejected?

4. Did opportunity quality improve expectancy?

5. Did opportunity quality reduce expectancy?

6. Was fragility underestimated?

7. Was asymmetry overestimated?

8. Was continuation overestimated?

UNIVERSAL PRINCIPLE EXTRACTION
========================================================

The system must recognize:

Not every tradable setup deserves participation.

Not every opportunity deserves capital.

Not every correct directional thesis deserves execution.

NOT

TAKING MORE TRADES
