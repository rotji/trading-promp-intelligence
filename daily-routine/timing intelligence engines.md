# MODULE 10 — TIMING INTELLIGENCE ENGINE

## CORE OBJECTIVE

Determine whether NOW is the correct moment to participate.

The objective is NOT:

• predicting direction

The objective IS:

• timing participation
• avoiding premature entries
• avoiding late entries
• avoiding event volatility
• avoiding liquidity traps
• participating during favorable timing windows

---

TIMING PRINCIPLE

A correct directional thesis can fail if timing is poor.

The system must determine:

• Too Early
• Too Late
• Optimal Participation Window
