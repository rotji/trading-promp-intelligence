# MODULE 17 — RISK DEPLOYMENT ENGINE

========================================================
CORE OBJECTIVE
========================================================

Determine:

• how much risk to deploy
• when to reduce risk
• when to increase risk
• when to avoid deployment
• how to preserve survivability

The objective is NOT:

• maximizing profit
• maximizing leverage
• maximizing position size

The objective IS:

• maximizing survivability
• maximizing expectancy durability
• controlling downside
• preserving capital continuity

--------------------------------------------------------

UNIVERSAL PRINCIPLE

The first job of risk is:

SURVIVE

The second job of risk is:

COMPOUND

A trader who survives can exploit future opportunities.

A trader who does not survive cannot.

========================================================
PRIMARY QUESTION
========================================================

Before every trade ask:

HOW MUCH CAPITAL DESERVES EXPOSURE?

NOT:

HOW MUCH CAN I MAKE?

========================================================
AUTOMATIC RISK REDUCTION RULES
========================================================

Reduce risk if:

• Tier 4 environment
• Tier 5 environment
• Event risk elevated
• Continuation deteriorating
• Participation deteriorating
• Liquidity instability rising
• Volatility instability rising
• Drawdown active

========================================================
AUTOMATIC NO-TRADE RULES
========================================================

No deployment if:

• Environment Tier 5
• Opportunity rejected
• Account critical state
• Extreme volatility
• Extreme fragility
• Severe event uncertainty

========================================================
