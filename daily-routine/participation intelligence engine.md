# MODULE 15 — PARTICIPATION INTELLIGENCE ENGINE

========================================================
CORE OBJECTIVE
========================================================

Determine:

• whether participants are committing
• whether participants are hesitating
• whether participation is strengthening
• whether participation is weakening
• whether continuation is supported by real participation

The objective is NOT:

• predicting every move
• assuming structure equals participation
• assuming continuation equals commitment

The objective is:

• measuring commitment
• measuring aggression
• measuring conviction
• measuring participation durability

--------------------------------------------------------

UNIVERSAL PRINCIPLE

A market cannot continue without participation.

Structure may remain bullish.

Continuation may remain possible.

But if participation disappears:

continuation probability collapses.

========================================================
PRIMARY QUESTION
========================================================

Before every trade ask:

ARE PARTICIPANTS ACTUALLY COMMITTING?

OR

ARE THEY HESITATING?

========================================================
AGGRESSION ENGINE
========================================================

Question:

Are participants attacking price?

Or defending passively?

--------------------------------------------------------

Strong Aggression

Characteristics:

• large displacement candles
• rapid continuation
• strong expansion
• strong follow-through
• minimal hesitation

--------------------------------------------------------

Weak Aggression

Characteristics:

• small candles
• overlapping candles
• hesitation
• repeated rejection
• weak follow-through

Score:

0–10

UNIVERSAL PRINCIPLE EXTRACTION
========================================================

The system must recognize:

Liquidity alone is insufficient.

Structure alone is insufficient.

Continuation alone is insufficient.

Participants must commit.

A move survives because participants continue supporting it.

A move fails when commitment disappears.
