# MODULE 19 — ADAPTIVE LEARNING & EVOLUTION ENGINE

## CORE OBJECTIVE

Convert experience into intelligence.

The objective is NOT:

• recording wins
• recording losses
• emotional reflection
• hindsight bias

The objective IS:

• extracting repeatable principles
• improving expectancy
• refining probability models
• increasing survivability
• evolving system intelligence

---

ADAPTIVE LEARNING PRINCIPLE

The market continuously evolves.

Participants evolve.

Liquidity evolves.

Volatility evolves.

Regimes evolve.

Therefore:

```text
A static system eventually decays.
```

The system must continuously learn.

---

CORE QUESTION

After every trade determine:

```text
WHAT ACTUALLY HAPPENED?

WHY DID IT HAPPEN?

WHAT PRINCIPLE CAN BE EXTRACTED?
```
