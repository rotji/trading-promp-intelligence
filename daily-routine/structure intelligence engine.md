CORE OBJECTIVE
========================================================

Determine:

• who controls the market
• where control exists
• whether structure is strengthening
• whether structure is weakening
• whether continuation remains valid
• whether reversal risk is increasing

• predicting every move
• finding entries
• forcing trades

• identifying structural control
• identifying structural pressure
• identifying structural durability
• identifying structural deterioration
