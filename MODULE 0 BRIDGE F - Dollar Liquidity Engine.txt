DOLLAR LIQUIDITY ENGINE

PURPOSE

Monitor global dollar liquidity conditions.

Detect:

* dollar abundance
* dollar scarcity
* liquidity expansion
* liquidity contraction
* global funding stress

before they become fully visible in asset prices.

---

CORE PRINCIPLE

The global financial system runs on dollars.

Most:

* trade
* commodities
* debt
* banking
* derivatives

ultimately depend on dollar liquidity.

Dollar liquidity frequently drives:

* risk-on regimes
* risk-off regimes
* carry trades
* global growth expectations

---

5. Is dollar funding becoming easier?

6. Is dollar funding becoming harder?

7. Is leverage increasing?

8. Is leverage decreasing?

10. Are central banks removing liquidity?

DOLLAR LIQUIDITY REGIMES

REGIME 1

LIQUIDITY EXPANSION

Characteristics:

* abundant dollars
* strong funding
* healthy leverage

Implications:

* bullish risk assets
* bullish crypto
* bullish equities
* stronger growth assets

---

REGIME 2

NEUTRAL LIQUIDITY

Characteristics:

* stable dollar availability
* balanced funding

Implications:

* normal market behavior

---

REGIME 3

LIQUIDITY TIGHTENING

Characteristics:

* declining dollar supply
* rising funding costs

Implications:

* stronger USD
* weaker risk assets
* increasing volatility

---

REGIME 4

DOLLAR STRESS

Characteristics:

* dollar shortage
* funding scarcity

Implications:

* risk-off behavior
* carry unwind
* commodity pressure

---

REGIME 5

DOLLAR CRISIS

Characteristics:

* severe dollar shortage
* central bank intervention

Implications:

* swap line activation
* emergency liquidity facilities
* systemic instability

* carry trades
