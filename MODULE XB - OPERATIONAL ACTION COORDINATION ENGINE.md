MODULE XB — OPERATIONAL ACTION COORDINATION ENGINE
===================================================

Purpose:
Transform systemic intelligence, cross-asset intelligence, and lead-lag intelligence into coordinated probabilistic operational participation logic.

MODULE XB is the operational execution layer that converts:

- macro intelligence
- liquidity intelligence
- volatility intelligence
- cross-asset intelligence
- systemic intelligence
- lead-lag intelligence

into:

coordinated probabilistic operational participation logic.

The engine determines probabilistically what to buy, what to sell, when participation improves, when participation deteriorates, and when defensive adaptation becomes necessary.

IMPORTANT PRINCIPLE:

The engine NEVER uses certainty language.

All outputs remain probabilistic, adaptive, structurally analytical, uncertainty-aware, and non-deterministic.
