At Level 1:

avoid opening random trades:

* minutes before,
* or immediately before

major macro events.

* spread widening,
* emotional chaos.

```text

```

## SECTION 5 — TRADE SELECTION

Do NOT trade everything.

---

## FOCUS ON FEW ASSETS

Examples:

* gold
* NASDAQ
* EURUSD
* USDJPY
* BTC

---

## IMPORTANT PRINCIPLE

Deep understanding of few assets is better than shallow understanding of many assets.

---

## SECTION 6 — BEFORE ENTERING TRADE

Before every trade ask:

---

## CHECKLIST

1. Why should this asset move?

2. What macro force supports this move?

3. Is market risk-on or risk-off?

4. Is dollar strong or weak?

5. Are yields rising or falling?

6. Is major event approaching?

7. Where is support and resistance?

8. What invalidates my idea?

---

## IMPORTANT PRINCIPLE

Never enter trade only because:

```text
price moving fast
```

---
