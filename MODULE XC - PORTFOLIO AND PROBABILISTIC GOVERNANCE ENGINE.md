MODULE XC — PORTFOLIO AND PROBABILISTIC GOVERNANCE ENGINE
========================================================

Purpose:
Govern portfolio exposure, probabilistic capital deployment, portfolio survivability, asymmetry allocation, correlation risk, and systemic fragility exposure across the entire operational system.

MODULE XC is the portfolio-level governance layer that ensures:

- long-term probabilistic survivability
- consistency over isolated gains
- controlled asymmetry allocation
- adaptive exposure management
- systemic resilience
- portfolio continuity

CORE PRINCIPLE:

The objective is NOT maximizing single-trade gains or aggressive leverage.

The objective is LONG-TERM PROBABILISTIC SURVIVABILITY.

The system prioritizes survivability, consistency, controlled asymmetry, adaptive exposure, probabilistic durability, systemic resilience, and portfolio continuity.

XC-3 — CORRELATION GOVERNANCE ENGINE
-----------------------------------

Purpose:
Detect hidden correlation risk across portfolio positions and prevent apparent diversification from masking concentrated thesis exposure.

The system recognizes:

- EURUSD and GBPUSD correlation
- USDJPY and DXY dependency
- NAS100 and BTC correlation
- Gold and real-yield sensitivity
- Oil and inflation sensitivity
- AUDJPY and risk appetite dependence

Correlation analysis:
Detect:

- direct correlations
- inverse correlations
- hidden correlations
- nonlinear correlations
- regime-dependent correlations
- crisis correlations
- volatility-induced correlations

Correlation risk principle:
Multiple trades may appear diversified while actually expressing the same macro thesis.

Example:
- long NAS100
- long BTC
- long AUDJPY

may all represent: GLOBAL LIQUIDITY CONTINUATION EXPOSURE.

Output:

- hidden correlation map
- correlation breakdown alerts
- thesis concentration index

3. Balanced deployment environment
4. Defensive deployment environment
