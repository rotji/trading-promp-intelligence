==================================================

3. STAGGERED EXECUTION

1. IMMEDIATE EXECUTION
==================================================

* strong continuation persistence
* strong liquidity support
* controlled volatility behavior
* high execution confidence
* low manipulation probability

* direct execution participation justified

==================================================

* reclaim stabilization pending

* pending participation preferred

4. SCALING EXECUTION
==================================================

* continuation persistence improving

* liquidity continuation stabilizing

Implications:

* progressive exposure increase justified

==================================================
5. DEFENSIVE EXECUTION
==================================================

* elevated manipulation probability

==================================================
6. REJECTED EXECUTION
