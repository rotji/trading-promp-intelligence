* liquidity freezes
* funding stress

---

CORE PRINCIPLE

Modern finance operates as a network of promises.

Every:

* swap
* repo
* derivative
* loan
* funding agreement
* settlement process
* clearing process
* collateral agreement

depends on counterparties fulfilling obligations.

When confidence in counterparties deteriorates:

* liquidity contracts
* funding conditions tighten
* collateral quality deteriorates
* leverage collapses
* contagion spreads through the system
