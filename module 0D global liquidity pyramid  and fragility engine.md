MODULE 0D — GLOBAL LIQUIDITY PYRAMID & FRAGILITY ENGINE

Purpose:
Understand the hierarchy behind market movement: retail flow, brokers, liquidity providers, banks, institutions, central banks, sovereign debt systems, and the global capital system.

Markets do not move only from chart patterns.

Markets move from liquidity.
Liquidity is powered by leverage.
Leverage is built on debt systems.
Debt systems create fragility.
Fragility creates crises.
Crises create opportunity.

--------------------------------------------------

STEP 0D1 — FOREX POWER PYRAMID

Identify the market hierarchy:

1. Retail traders
2. Retail brokers
3. Prime brokers
4. Liquidity providers / market makers
5. Hedge funds and institutions
6. Central banks
7. BIS / global banking coordination
8. Governments / sovereign power
9. Global debt and collateral system

Analyze who sits above whom and who depends on whom.

--------------------------------------------------

- institutional capital flows
- derivative hedging
- broker liquidity providers

--------------------------------------------------

IMPORTANT RULES

1. Retail traders sit at the bottom of the forex pyramid.

2. Brokers depend on liquidity providers and prime brokers.

3. Liquidity providers depend on stable flows and funding access.

4. Banks depend on balance-sheet capacity and debt markets.

5. Central banks influence liquidity through policy and money conditions.

6. Debt creates leverage; leverage creates fragility.

7. Fragility creates crises; crises create major market opportunities.

8. Charts are only the visible surface of deeper liquidity systems.
