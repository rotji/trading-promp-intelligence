SYSTEMIC RISK ENGINE

PURPOSE

Integrate all major funding, liquidity, credit, derivatives, and positioning systems into a single framework for detecting:

* systemic instability
* crisis probability
* liquidity breakdown
* contagion risk
* leverage vulnerability

before those risks become fully visible in market prices.

CORE PRINCIPLE

Major financial crises rarely emerge from a single event.

Crises emerge when multiple systems begin failing simultaneously.

The objective of this engine is to detect:

* convergence of stress
* correlation of weakness
* contagion pathways

before full crisis conditions emerge.
