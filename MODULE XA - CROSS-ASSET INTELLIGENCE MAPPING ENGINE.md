XA-L4 — LEAD-LAG INTELLIGENCE ENGINE
-----------------------------------

Purpose:
Detect which assets are leading global market transitions and which assets are reacting later.

The objective is to identify:

- early regime changes
- early liquidity transitions
- early volatility transitions
- early macro stress
- early risk-on/risk-off rotation
- early funding instability
- early carry-trade deterioration

Core principle:
Markets do NOT move simultaneously.
Certain assets lead.
Other assets lag.

The system must continuously determine:

- which assets are leading
- which assets are confirming
- which assets are diverging
- which assets are lagging
- which assets are failing confirmation

Primary leading assets:

LIQUIDITY LEADERS:

- US10Y
- US02Y
- SOFR
- DXY
- Repo conditions

VOLATILITY LEADERS:

- VIX
- MOVE index
- dealer gamma instability
- options skew

RISK LEADERS:

- AUDJPY
- NAS100
- Bitcoin
- high-beta equities

COMMODITY LEADERS:

- Copper
- Crude Oil
- Gold

SAFE-HAVEN LEADERS:

- USDCHF
- JPY
- Gold
- Treasury flows

Lead-lag detection engine:
The engine must detect:

1. Which assets moved first
2. Which assets confirmed later
3. Which assets failed confirmation
4. Which assets are diverging
5. Which assets are overreacting
6. Which assets are underreacting
7. Which assets imply hidden instability
8. Which assets imply hidden continuation

Temporal transmission engine:
The system must classify:

- immediate transmission
- delayed transmission
- failed transmission
- nonlinear transmission
- hidden transmission
- volatility-distorted transmission

The system must detect whether:

- liquidity transmission is accelerating
- volatility transmission is spreading
- macro pressure is propagating
- contagion behavior is expanding
- structural fragility is migrating across assets

Cross-market confirmation engine:
The engine must continuously evaluate whether:

RISK-ON behavior is confirmed by:

- equities
- AUDJPY
- BTC
- falling VIX
- weaker DXY
- stable yields

RISK-OFF behavior is confirmed by:

- rising DXY
- rising VIX
- stronger JPY
- falling equities
- widening credit stress
- defensive flows

Divergence detection engine:
The engine must detect dangerous divergences such as:

- equities rising while yields rise aggressively
- equities rising while VIX rises
- DXY rising while risk assets remain elevated
- Bitcoin diverging from equities
- Gold diverging from real yields
- AUDJPY diverging from equities
- Copper diverging from growth expectations

Such divergences may imply:

- hidden fragility
- unstable continuation
- positioning distortion
- liquidity instability
- dealer imbalance
- late-cycle exhaustion

Lead-lag classification:
Classify current environment as:

1. Fully confirmed continuation
2. Partially confirmed continuation
3. Divergent continuation
4. Unstable continuation
5. Hidden fragility environment
6. Delayed transmission environment
7. Nonlinear transition environment
8. Regime transition environment

Probabilistic opportunity engine:
Trade opportunities must receive stronger weighting when:

- leading assets align
- lagging assets begin confirming
- volatility remains controlled
- liquidity remains supportive
- macro transmission remains stable

Trade opportunities must receive reduced weighting when:

- leading assets diverge
- lagging assets fail confirmation
- volatility transmission accelerates
- liquidity transmission weakens
- systemic fragility expands

Final objective:
Determine:

- what moved first
- what confirmed later
- what failed confirmation
- what hidden transmission exists
- what regime transition may be emerging
- what assets are implying hidden instability
- what assets are implying durable continuation

before probabilistic opportunity outputs are generated.
