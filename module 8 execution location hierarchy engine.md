==================================================

EXECUTION LOCATION HIERARCHY ENGINE
==================================================

PURPOSE
==================================================

The purpose of this module is to classify whether
current execution location possesses:

* structural efficiency
* asymmetry efficiency
* liquidity efficiency
* continuation durability
* volatility stability
* reclaim quality
* expansion quality
* exhaustion sensitivity
* liquidity trap sensitivity
* execution fragility

before directional execution assumptions are approved.

The objective is NOT directional prediction.

The objective is determining whether the current
location justifies probabilistic risk deployment.

==================================================
CORE EXECUTION LOCATION PRINCIPLE
==================================================

Execution quality depends heavily on location quality.

Correct directional bias executed at poor structural
locations may still produce low-quality trades.

High-quality execution locations improve:

* asymmetry quality
* invalidation efficiency
* continuation durability
* reward/risk structure
* volatility survivability
* liquidity efficiency

before trade participation occurs.

==================================================
PRIMARY LOCATION CLASSIFICATION ENGINE
==================================================

The system must classify whether current price
location belongs to:

1. HIGH-ASYMMETRY LOCATION
2. CONFIRMATION LOCATION
3. EXHAUSTION LOCATION
4. LIQUIDITY TRAP LOCATION
5. STRUCTURAL RECLAIM LOCATION

before execution participation is approved.

==================================================
1. HIGH-ASYMMETRY LOCATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* reclaim stabilization emerging
* liquidity absorption visible
* continuation potential improving
* volatility compression improving
* invalidation distance controlled
* structural support/resistance nearby
* early expansion potential present

Implications:

* elevated reward/risk efficiency
* tighter structural invalidation possible
* elevated continuation asymmetry
* improved expansion potential

Risk Characteristics:

* lower confirmation quality
* elevated execution sensitivity
* elevated timing sensitivity

==================================================
2. CONFIRMATION LOCATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* continuation already confirmed
* displacement behavior visible
* reclaim behavior validated
* liquidity persistence improving
* volatility stability acceptable
* follow-through persistence visible

Implications:

* balanced execution quality
* balanced asymmetry
* balanced continuation durability
* moderate expansion potential

Risk Characteristics:

* reduced asymmetry efficiency
* wider invalidation tolerance required

==================================================
3. EXHAUSTION LOCATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* extended expansion behavior
* vertical continuation acceleration
* asymmetry compression visible
* elevated reversal sensitivity
* elevated sweep probability
* elevated exhaustion probability

Implications:

* continuation durability deteriorating
* elevated nonlinear reversal sensitivity
* reduced reward/risk efficiency

Risk Characteristics:

* elevated continuation fragility
* elevated liquidity reversal sensitivity

==================================================
4. LIQUIDITY TRAP LOCATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* obvious breakout participation zone
* unresolved expansion instability
* elevated false-break probability
* elevated liquidity sweep sensitivity
* unstable continuation persistence
* emotional crowd participation risk

Implications:

* elevated execution fragility
* elevated volatility instability
* elevated continuation failure probability

Risk Characteristics:

* elevated stop-sweep sensitivity
* elevated dealer-flow instability

==================================================
5. STRUCTURAL RECLAIM LOCATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* failed breakdown recovery
* reclaim stabilization emerging
* liquidity absorption confirmed
* displacement restoration visible
* continuation restoration improving
* volatility instability compressing

Implications:

* improved continuation durability
* improved structural persistence
* improved asymmetry quality

Risk Characteristics:

* reclaim failure sensitivity remains active
* continuation requires persistence confirmation

==================================================
EXECUTION LOCATION VARIABLES
==================================================

The system must prioritize:

* liquidity behavior
* reclaim behavior
* structural support/resistance
* volatility compression
* displacement quality
* continuation persistence
* expansion stage
* sweep behavior
* higher timeframe inheritance
* liquidity concentration
* dealer-flow behavior

before isolated technical signals.

==================================================
EXECUTION LOCATION REJECTION CONDITIONS
==================================================

Execution quality deteriorates when:

* expansion already overextended
* volatility instability accelerating
* liquidity conditions deteriorating
* continuation persistence weakening
* asymmetry compressed materially
* reclaim confirmation absent
* timeframe conflict increasing
* liquidity sweep probability elevated

==================================================
STRUCTURAL LOCATION PRIORITY PRINCIPLE
==================================================

Execution quality improves when participation occurs:

* near reclaim stabilization
* near structural support restoration
* near volatility compression-release zones
* near liquidity absorption zones
* near asymmetry-efficient regions

Execution quality deteriorates when participation occurs:

* mid-range without asymmetry
* during emotional breakout chasing
* during unresolved volatility expansion
* during unstable liquidity behavior
* during late-stage continuation acceleration

==================================================
LOCATION QUALITY CLASSIFICATION
==================================================

The system must classify execution location quality as:

1. ELITE LOCATION
2. STRONG LOCATION
3. MODERATE LOCATION
4. FRAGILE LOCATION
5. REJECT LOCATION

==================================================
ELITE LOCATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* strong asymmetry quality
* strong reclaim structure
* strong liquidity efficiency
* strong invalidation efficiency
* controlled volatility behavior

Implications:

* strongest execution quality
* strongest expansion efficiency

==================================================
STRONG LOCATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* favorable continuation structure
* acceptable asymmetry quality
* manageable volatility behavior
* stable liquidity behavior

Implications:

* acceptable execution quality
* balanced continuation participation

==================================================
MODERATE LOCATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* mixed continuation quality
* moderate asymmetry quality
* elevated volatility sensitivity
* imperfect structural alignment

Implications:

* reduced execution aggressiveness required

==================================================
FRAGILE LOCATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* unstable continuation behavior
* deteriorating liquidity conditions
* elevated volatility instability
* elevated exhaustion sensitivity

Implications:

* defensive execution only

==================================================
REJECT LOCATION
==================================================

Characteristics:

* severe instability
* severe asymmetry deterioration
* severe volatility instability
* severe liquidity deterioration
* severe continuation fragility

Implications:

* participation discouraged

==================================================
FINAL OBJECTIVE
==================================================

The objective is determining whether:

* current execution location justifies participation
* asymmetry quality remains favorable
* liquidity conditions support persistence
* volatility conditions support survivability
* reclaim quality supports continuation
* structural durability supports expansion

before entry trigger construction begins.
