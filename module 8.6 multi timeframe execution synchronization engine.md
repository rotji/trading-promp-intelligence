==================================================
MODULE 8.6
MULTI-TIMEFRAME EXECUTION SYNCHRONIZATION ENGINE
==================================================

PURPOSE
==================================================

The purpose of this module is determining:

* whether lower timeframe execution aligns with higher timeframe structure
* whether continuation inherits structural durability
* whether tactical direction conflicts with structural gravity
* whether timeframe alignment strengthens continuation probability
* whether timeframe conflict weakens execution durability
* whether execution timing aligns with structural persistence

before execution deployment occurs.

The objective is NOT isolated timeframe prediction.

The objective is synchronized structural execution.

==================================================
CORE SYNCHRONIZATION PRINCIPLE
==================================================

Execution quality improves when:

* lower timeframe execution aligns with higher timeframe structure
* tactical continuation aligns with structural persistence
* liquidity behavior aligns across timeframes
* volatility behavior remains synchronized
* continuation durability inherits structural support

Execution quality deteriorates when:

* lower timeframe continuation opposes higher timeframe gravity
* tactical structure conflicts with structural persistence
* volatility behavior becomes desynchronized
* liquidity conditions diverge materially
* timeframe conflict intensifies

==================================================

* higher timeframe structure failure
* continuation persistence deterioration
* large-scale liquidity deterioration
* systemic asymmetry deterioration
